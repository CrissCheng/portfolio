About Me

Personal Details

Hola!, my name is Criss. I'm a UX researcher passionate about the intersection of technology and human-centric design.

I have an innate curiosity of learning about people and a passion for designing human experiences. I believe that a good design is the combination of empathy, creativity and collaboration. Research is my jam.

Previously, I worked at Shopify and UXTesting. I also helped several startups like Jobalaya and VoiceTube with freelance projects. I have experiences conducting research studies across different industries and participated in various stages of product development. You could learn more about my work process in one of the blog that I wrote here.

Outside work, I'm a big fan of travel, photography (my photography, no scam), and surfing. And I'm always learning about new topics in data science, UX and psychology (and technology, if not being too general).

My Process

User Research

I seek to understand baviors, patterns and underlying needs. I thrive to design products using both qualitative and quatitative methods. I have helped B2B, biotech, and mobile app startups develope product strategy using my user-centered approach.

Content Strategy

After defining the strategy and scope of the product, I introduce information architecture components to help develope the structure and skeleton of the prodcut. I work on organization, labeling, navigation, and searching to present the foundation of a good user experience.

Wireframe

From sketch to hi-fi prototype, I love prototype as a way to communicate and validate ideas. I believe a good prototype invites inspiration, evolvement and validation. An investment in early prototyping saves the time of building unusable software.

Product Design

Taking direction from empathy-based research, my approach to user centered design constitute ideation, prototyping, UI design, and testing. Iteration is the foundation of a good design.
